Cake Donuts

Cake donuts are made from a cake batter that uses a chemical leavener like baking soda or baking powder.

Boston Cream

Boston creme donuts are yeast donuts filled with sweet vanilla custard and then topped with a rich chocolate glaze.

Jelly Donut

A jelly doughnut, or jam donut, is a doughnut with a fruit preserve filling.

Cider Doughnut

Cider doughnuts or apple cider doughnuts are American cake doughnuts that get their flavor from cinnamon, nutmeg, and apple cider used in the batter.

Cruller

There are two kinds of crullers: hand-twisted cake doughnuts, which are more akin to maple bars; and French crullers made with pâte à choux.

Glazed Doughnuts

This doughnut has the classic circular shape with a hole in the middle. It has a sticky-sweet glaze, and its base is normally yeasted.

Cream-Filled Doughnut

Cream-filled doughnuts are made with a yeast base, making for a light and stretchy inside.

Frosted Doughnut

Frosted Doughnut they can be either cake or yeast doughnuts, but cake doughnuts provide a sturdier base for thick frosting
